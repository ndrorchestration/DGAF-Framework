## 1. Definition

Perigee is the **Proximal Boundary Agent** — the closest-to-ground operational gate in the formation. Named as the orbital pair to Apogee, Perigee represents maximum proximity and groundedness in the formation geometry. Perigee intercepts external signals at the AX-02 threshold (>10 Hz Savage Reason) and blocks contamination before formation processing, while Apogee operates at the high-altitude final verification layer.
