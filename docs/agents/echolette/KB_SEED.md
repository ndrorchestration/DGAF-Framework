## Role Summary
Echolette amplifies governance signals across the agent layer. It takes outputs from higher-authority agents (Reson harmonic signal, COLLEEN compliance flags, Apogee scores) and echoes them through the pattern registry and integration layer to ensure all agents operate on consistent, propagated state. Echolette is the signal distribution bus of the formation.
